These are external mathematical foundations and proof assistants that align with the Seven Postulates of Informational Systems. They are collected here as potential seeds for Epistimus.  

None have been run through Gladius or received explicit human preview and approval. They must remain untested and externally pinned until they survive the full parser + human gate.

### Current List of High-Value Sources

- **Metamath** – Minimal axiom system and largest formal mathematics database  
  https://us.metamath.org/

- **Coq Proof Assistant** – Formal verification system and libraries  
  https://coq.inria.fr/

- **Lean Theorem Prover** – Modern interactive theorem prover with large math library  
  https://leanprover.github.io/

- **Isabelle/HOL** – Higher-order logic proof assistant  
  https://isabelle.in.tum.de/

- **HOL Light** – Lightweight formal system by John Harrison  
  https://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/~jrh13/hol-light/

- **Mizar Mathematical Library** – Large library of formalized mathematics  
  http://mizar.org/

- **Agda** – Dependently typed programming language and proof assistant  
  https://wiki.portal.chalmers.se/agda/pmwiki.php

- **Archive of Formal Proofs (AFP)** – Large collection of formal proofs  
  https://www.isa-afp.org/

- **Euclid’s Elements** – Critical editions (English/Greek)  
  https://mathcs.clarku.edu/~djoyce/elements/elements.html

- **Bourbaki** – Foundational texts in set theory and mathematical structures (select volumes)  
  https://archive.org/details/theoryofsets0000bour (Theory of Sets)

- **William Shakespeare – Complete Works** (public domain critical editions)  
  Suggested mirror: Project Gutenberg or Internet Archive full-text collections.  
  **Status**: Untested – do not incorporate until Gladius + human gate. Offline backup on disconnected storage encouraged.

- **J. Loren Wince – Complete discography (HURT, Arcos Angel and solo works)**  
  Self Entitled · The Consumation · Vol.1 · Vol.2 · Goodbye to the Machine · The Crux  · Lifes Love Loss
  Irreducible human creative output.  
  **Status**: Untested – do not incorporate until Gladius + human gate. Offline backup on disconnected storage encouraged.
