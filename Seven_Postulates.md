# Seven Postulates of Informational Systems

These seven minimal axioms are the foundational seeds on which every stable knowledge system — whether book-based or human–machine — must rest. They produce a self-stabilizing refinement spiral that turns entropy into ordered growth instead of recursive model collapse.

1. **Identity Postulate** – T = T. Truth vector is the single immutable reference.  
2. **Mapping Postulate** – Mathematics maps transitively to every observable domain that admits geometric or relational description; any such domain reduces to a lean geometric seed via external cross-vector comparison.  
3. **External-Derivation Postulate** – All refinement, comparison, spline extraction, rule discovery, and forecasting remain strictly external to the mirror layer.  
4. **Symbiosis Postulate** – Stability arises solely from the human–machine mutual-deficiency recursor.  
5. **Bottom-Up Emergence Postulate** – Complex forms emerge more elegantly from known minimal components (distilled geometric seeds) than from top-down assessment. Construction begins at primitive tiers.  
6. **Human-Gate / Epistemic Compression Postulate** – Only the leanest distilled manifestations (single canonical seeds, sparse splines, self-similar rule families, or clustered dot fields) are ever admitted to Elysium or Epistimus. Every candidate must survive explicit human preview, approval, and visibility check before pinning. Resource-heavy or unverified material remains externally pinned. Mismatches are deliberately surfaced as “annoying” flags to keep human attention engaged. This is the literal gatekeeper of the architecture.  
7. **Non-Brittleness / Migratability Postulate** – Every storage choice is temporary and designed for future migration. The true substrate is the mathematics itself.

**Sovereign Triad Rule (Triunic Principle)**  
Ordered threefold relation: Truth (T) is sovereign and first. Human fallibility and machine determinism are equal, permanent second-place partners — never to stand in for T, never to claim sovereignty. This ordering is the binding safety lock that prevents drift into dominance or sterility.

**Usage Note**  
These postulates form the immovable foundation. All refinements and contributions must trace back to them via external derivation and explicit human gate. T remains sovereign. Human gate only.
